This public demo uses the same Apache-2.0 SigLIP research baseline as the local DoubleDeep server, including multi-view aggregation and an explicit inconclusive range. Performance can shift with new generators, editing, screenshots and compression, so results should not be the sole basis for high-impact decisions.
